## Yucatan 2023 GHG Inventory

- **Source document**: Inventario Estatal de Emisiones de Gases y Compuestos de Efecto Invernadero del Estado de Yucatan, 2010-2023
- **Producer**: Secretaria de Desarrollo Sustentable (SDS), Gobierno del Estado de Yucatan
- **Consultancy**: Centro Mario Molina para Estudios Estrategicos Sobre Energia y Medio Ambiente A.C.
- **Date published**: 2024
- **Local filename**: IEEGYCEI_del_Estado_de_Yucatan_2010_2023.pdf
- **GWPs**: AR5 (CH4=28, N2O=265) -- NO CONVERSION NEEDED
- **Methodology**: IPCC 2006 Guidelines + 2019 Refinement
- **Coverage**: Full state territory; all IPCC sectors; 2010-2023 annual time series
- **Gases**: CO2, CH4, N2O, HFC, HCFC, PFC, SF6, NF3, carbon negro (CN)
- **Key uncertainty**: +/-5.78% overall (without absorptions)

### Key data tables used
- **Table 1** (p.6): Emissions by gas type -- confirms AR5 GWPs
- **Table 8** (pp.24-26): Trend 2010-2023 by sector/category
- **Table 9** (pp.30-35): Complete 2023 emissions by IPCC category with CO2/CH4/N2O/HFC/HCFC breakdown
- **Table 12** (p.43): Electricity generation fuel consumption in PJ, 2010-2023
- **Figure 5** (p.29): Sector breakdown sunburst chart

### Validation notes
- Total 2023 (excl. 3B+3D): 10,425.52 GgCO2e (Table 8/9 header)
- Net emissions: -2,685.91 GgCO2e (net carbon sink due to forest absorption)
- Energy sector: 7,416.84 GgCO2e (71.14% of total)
- IPPU: 682.56 GgCO2e (6.55%)
- AFOLU (sin 3B+3D): 1,543.41 GgCO2e (14.80%)
- Waste: 782.71 GgCO2e (7.51%)
- CO2: 72.02%, CH4: 17.99%, N2O: 6.78%, F-gases: 3.21%
- HFC: 331.81 GgCO2e, HCFC: 2.37 GgCO2e -- BOTH QUANTIFIED
- Electricity generation: 93.5% natural gas, 4.1% diesel, 2.4% combustoleo (2023)
- Cement production (2A1): 318.04 GgCO2e -- single large facility (CEMEX Merida)
- Per capita: 4.40 tCO2e/hab (below national average of 5.57)

### Quality assessment
This is the highest-quality state inventory in the project:
1. Already uses AR5 GWPs (no conversion needed)
2. 2023 data (most recent of any state)
3. Full annual time series 2010-2023
4. HFCs quantified by application type
5. Detailed manufacturing subsector breakdown
6. Fuel consumption data for electricity generation
7. INECC-reviewed for quality assurance
